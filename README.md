## About Zaim Tech

Zaim Tech is a young and fast-growing technology company founded in Kenya with a clear mission: to use technology to create solutions, inspire innovation, and empower the next generation.

The company was founded by Zaim Maalim Sheali, a 15-year-old tech innovator from Shimanzi, Mombasa, Kenya. At a very young age, he showed strong interest in computers, software, and modern technology. Instead of waiting for opportunities, he started creating them.

Zaim Tech focuses on:

Software and app development

Technology solutions and digital projects

Media and streaming technology

Learning and sharing tech knowledge

Despite his young age, Zaim Maalim Sheali has worked on many technology projects, learning through practice, research, and self-study. His journey proves that passion, discipline, and hard work are more important than age.

Zaim Tech was created to solve real problems, help people through technology, and show that young minds can build powerful systems. The company also aims to inspire youth in Kenya and across Africa to believe in their ideas and start early.

This is only the beginning of the Zaim Tech story.
The vision is big. The future is digital.
